Terms & Conditions

By accessing the Partner Portal or residing within The Imperial, you agree to abide by the following operational and ethical standards.

1. Acceptance of Services

The Imperial provides an integrated living ecosystem powered by PropManage OS. Access to this ecosystem is granted exclusively to verified residents, owners, and authorized staff members. Unauthorized access attempts trigger immediate security protocols.

2. Automated Billing & Payments

All service charges, utility bills, and rental payments are processed through the Partner Portal. Residents agree to maintain active payment methods. Late payments may result in the automated suspension of non-essential smart-home features.

3. Security & Biometrics

Usage of the building's facilities (Cinema, Gym, Pools) requires biometric verification. Residents consent to the use of facial recognition for access control purposes within the communal areas of the property.

4. Property Maintenance

IoT sensors within units monitor critical infrastructure. While we respect your physical privacy, the system will automatically raise maintenance tickets for life-safety issues (gas leaks, pipe bursts) which grant emergency access to our 24/7 technical team.

5. Modification of Terms

Imperial Inc. reserves the right to modify these terms as building operations evolve. Any significant changes will be broadcasted via the Notice Board within the Partner Portal.
